What is Deep Learning

Deep learning is a subset of Machine Learning that involves the use of artificial neural networks to analyze and interpret data. It is based on the idea that a machine can be trained to learn and improve its performance on a task by exposing it to a large amount of data, rather than relying on a set of predetermined rules or algorithms.

What can Deep Learning do?


  • Computer Vision: Deep learning algorithms can be used to recognize objects in images and videos, classify images, detect and track objects, and more.
  • language processing : Deep learning can be used to translate text from one language to another.
  • Bioinformatics : Deep learning can be used in bioinformatics to analyze and interpret biological data, such as DNA sequences.
  • Drug Design : Deep learning can be used in drug design to predict the effectiveness of potential drugs and identify potential side effects.
  • Climate Science : Deep learning can be used in climate science to analyze and predict weather patterns and climate change.
